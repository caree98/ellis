For organizers

Run a scorecard drive.

A short playbook for getting a group of buildings scored at once.

Step 1

Recruit 3 to 5 building leads

One contact per building is enough. They don't need technical background — the scorecard walks them through it.

15 min Step 2

Schedule a 60-minute kickoff

Walk through the scorecard live, demo the priority-list output, answer questions. Use our slide kit.

60 min Step 3

Aggregate and prioritize

Roll up the scores by category. The buildings with the lowest GUV or filtration scores become the next letter-generator runs.

2 hours Step 4

Send the letters

Use the letter generator with each building's results. Send as a coordinated batch — singular asks get ignored; group asks get a meeting.

3 hours
