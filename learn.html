Where to verify.

Every claim on Ellis points back to one of these primary sources.

ASHRAE — Standards 62.1 (ventilation), 52.2 (filtration), 185.1 (UVGI), and the Indoor Air Quality Guide.

CDC — Ventilation guidance for schools, businesses, and healthcare settings; infection-control guidance for airborne pathogens.

NIOSH — Respiratory protection guidance, including fit-testing and respirator selection.

Corsi-Rosenthal box — the open-source DIY filtration design and the field-measurement studies behind it.
