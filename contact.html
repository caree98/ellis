Partnerships

Work with us.

Ellis is a public-good project. We partner with schools, health departments, and research organizations.

If you're running a school-district scorecard drive, a workplace ventilation audit, or a community-health pilot — we'd like to hear from you. Partnership bandwidth is limited, but we prioritize organizations that can field-test tools and share findings.

Reach out to partners@ellis.example with a short description of your project and timeline.
