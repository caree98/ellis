Score, plan, monitor.

Self-assessments and calculators that give you a number or a plan you can act on — not a recommendation to go figure it out yourself. Every output is traceable back to ASHRAE, CDC, or NIOSH guidance.

01 / Assess

Building scorecard

Twelve questions across five categories — ventilation, filtration, GUV, monitoring, readiness. Get a score for each, not one number hiding the gaps.

GUV deployment engine

Enter your room dimensions and we'll estimate the upper-room UV fixture count, placement, and cost to hit your target air-change rate.
