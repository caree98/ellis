Tool 02 / Plan · Coming soon

GUV deployment engine.

Estimate the upper-room UV fixture count, placement, and cost to hit your target air-change rate. The engine is in development — this page is a placeholder for the working prototype.

What it'll do

Inputs and outputs.

The eventual interface, for reference.

Inputs

Room dimensions, ceiling height, target equivalent ACH

Plus a selection of fixture model (luminaire type, wattage, irradiance pattern).

4 fields
Outputs

Fixture count, wall placement grid, cost estimate

Plus a confidence range based on the irradiance model used. Citations to ASHRAE 185.1 inline.

3 panels
Constraints

Upper-room only, ≥ 2.4 m ceiling height, occupied-space eye-safety verified

The engine refuses to recommend in-duct or bare-lamp fixtures. Both require an engineer on-site.
