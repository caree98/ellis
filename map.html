About this data

Sourcing and limits.

What the map shows and what it doesn't.

United States. Data from the CDC National Syndromic Surveillance Program (NSSP). Shows the percentage of emergency department visits with a diagnosis of COVID-19, Influenza, or RSV. Updated weekly. Not all states report in all weeks.

World. Headlines from WHO Disease Outbreak News. Where a country has not published recent data, the cell is left uncolored — this is a data gap, not an absence of disease.

Not a clinical tool. Surveillance signal is for situational awareness. It does not predict local exposure, replace testing, or substitute for a healthcare provider.
